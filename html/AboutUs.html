Darius Owens

My name is Darius Owens. I'm currently on track to become a software developer. I come from a very diverse background. Before joining Code Fellows to become a software developer I was in the U.S. Navy where I worked as a weatherman. When my enlistment ended I worked in the security industry, insurance sales, car sales, and even delivered mail for the USPS. One of my biggest strengths I bring to the table is my ability to work with any person from any walk of life.
